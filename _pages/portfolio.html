Analyzing Team and Player Performance in the 2023 Cricket World Cup

This project examines the performance of teams and players in the 2023 Cricket World Cup, focusing on key aspects such as batting and bowling statistics, wicket dismissal types, and venue impact. As one of the most prestigious international cricket tournaments, the World Cup brings together top teams from around the globe. The 2023 edition, hosted in India, featured ten teams competing across multiple venues.
